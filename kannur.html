Kannur

Kannur is a one of the 14 districts of Kerala State in India. It is also termed as Cannanore , the town of Kannur is the headquarters of the District Kannur. Kannur District is bounded by Kasaragod District to the north, Kozhikode District to the south and Wayanad District to the south-east. To the east the district is bounded by the Western Ghats, which forms the border with Karnataka State, in its district of Kodagu. The Arabian Sea lies to the west.Kannur District is known as the land of looms and lores, because of the loom industries functioning in the district and festivals held in temples. The district is a major centre of Theyyam, a ritual dance of northern Kerala.

Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary

Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary was established in the year of 1984 by separating regions from the Odanthode Malavaram of Thalassery special division which was formerly a private forest. It is located at a distance of 50 Km from Thalassery and 66 Km from Kannur. It is spread over an area of 55 sq km.The Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary is home that has vast variety of flora and fauna within the Sanctuary.The highest peak is Katti Betta which is 1145m. high from the sea level. The most superior Time to visit the Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary in the month of September to March.Although it can be visited throughout the year.The annual rainfall around Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary is about 3,000 mm.

The sanctuary lies between latitudes 11o 54 and 11o 59 north latitudes and 75o 47 and 75o 57 east longitudes.Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary is one of famous tourist attraction that fascinating the people from the whole world.

How to reach?
- The nearest railway station is located at Thalassery, about 50 km.
- Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary is well connected by road network. The area is accessible by road from Thalassery, Kannur, Wayanad and Virajpet.
- Karipur International Airport, Kozhikode about 71 km from Thalasserry town.

St. Angelo Fort

St. Angelo Fort which is also known as Kannur Fort or Kannur, is located quite away from the town of Kannur, a city in Kerala is a fort facing the Arabian Sea. Today, St. Angelo’s Fort is a protected monument under the Archaeological Survey of India. The fort offers a fascinating view of the Moppila Bay and Dharmadom Island. The fort is located about 3 Km from Kannur city. The fort is one of great historical importance and tourist destination of Kerala State of India.

Dharmadom Island, only 5 acres in area, is situated 100 meters away from the mainland in the Arabian Sea. The Moppila Bay is a natural fishing bay. A sea wall projecting from the fort separates the rough sea and inland water. Today, the bay has turned into a modern fishing harbor, developed under the Indo-Norwegian Pact. The St. Angelo Fort is now under Archaeological Survey of India.

How to reach?
- Kannur railway station is well connected to other railway station of the state and other cities of India
- Kozhikode International Airport and Mangalore International Airport are the nearest airports to Kannur fort.
- Kannur has an extensive road network that connect it with other cities of Kerala and other state.

Muzhappilangad Drive In Beach

Muzhappilangad Beach is one of the most popular beaches of Kerala located in Kannur District of Kerala.Muzhappilangad Beach is the only Drive-in Beaches in Asia and the most popular beach in the Malabar region in Kerala. The beach is very beautiful and its 5km long long shore makes it a unique in itself. It is an ideal place to relax swim, take a sun bathe, or walking around the charming beach. Beach gives the capitivating beauty of the Arabian sea. Tourist can drive on this beach with there own vehicles and enjoy the scenic view of the beach.

Muzhappilangad beach also offers adventure sports like Parasailing, Paragliding, Micro-lite flights along with a range of water sports. Approximately 100–200 m south of the beach there is a private island called Dharmadam Island.

How to reach?
- Calicut International Airport is the nearest airport located abotu 98 km from here.
- Thalassery Rilway Station is located about 7 km from here. Kannur Railway Station is also located about 15 km from here.
- Thalassery and Kannur are the nearest town to the beach hence reaching here is very easy through road.
